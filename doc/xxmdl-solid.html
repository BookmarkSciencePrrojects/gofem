package solid implements models for solids based on continuum mechanics 

*            |    Rate
*  ============================================
*            |
*            | dσdt = f(σ,dεdt)
*    Small   | σ_(n+1) = σ_(n) + Δt * f_(n+1)
*            | StressUpdate
*            | D = dσ/dε_(n+1)
*            | ConsistentD
*            |
*  --------------------------------------------
*            |
*    Large   | dσdt = f(σ,F,dFdt)
*            | D = dσdF_(n+1)
